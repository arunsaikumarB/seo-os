# FAQ — Closed Beta

**How do I join beta?** Admin enables Beta Mode or accepts an invite code on `/org/beta`.

**Where do I report bugs?** `/org/feedback` — include severity and screenshot URL when possible.

**How fast should onboarding feel?** Under 15 minutes to first scan + opportunity view.

**Is Billing live?** No — out of scope until after 1.0 approval.

**What about Ahrefs/Semrush?** Out of scope for this platform phase.
